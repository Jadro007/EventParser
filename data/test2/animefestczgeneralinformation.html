cosplayer, porotce (2017)

2017 was an incredible first experience of Animefest. All the staff worked tremendously to put together an event that was fun, educational and breath taking whilst also showcasing the talent of local artists and cosplayers alike.

Aonir

cosplayer, porotce (2016)

I have been at Animefest 2016 and it is still one of my favorite convention memories ever. It is very well organized, the program and activity offers are numerous and I've never met so many kind and open hearted people/cosplayer somewhere else! I can highly recommend going to Animefest in beautiful Brno. ♥
Facebook

Kenny Cosplay Stuff

cosplayer, porotce (2017)

I'm most impressed with the organisation of the cosplay show. The format of the show where the cosplayer answers a few questions after the performance worked extremely well and it was very entertaining. Everyone there were amazing and the staff was super friendly.
Facebook

Zel COShorse

cosplayer, porotce (2015, 2017)

I absolutely love Animefest! Always have great time in Brno, it’s a lovely city, great conplace and fantastic people. Everybody- the organisers, the fans, the guests are so into that convention experience that you just can’t help but to sink in yourself. Really looking forward for my next visit!
Facebook

Papa Cosplay

cosplayer, porotce (2018)

Had a wonderfull experience at Animefest! Did my first streamed conference, the infrastructure is amazing: online streaming, several stages, amphitheatre with rotating stage (yes!) for cosplay contests, etc… The staff animated their convention through storytelling and factions! Adding the local artisans and game space, Animefest is perfectly designed for entertainment!

Eye of Sauron Designs

cosplayer, porotce (2018)

Animefest was great fun to me, not only because I got to meet some of my cosplay friends again, but also because I got the chance to meet so many amazing new people. There were so many great cosplayers, and I'm glad I got to judge the contest! I also wanna thank everyone who came to my panel and asked me questions, also the people who visited me at my booth and chatted with me. I hope I can come back sometime, because Animefest overall was great, and a fantastic experience!
